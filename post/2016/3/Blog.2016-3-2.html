Tales From The Mountains Pt. 2: Perseverance

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 2st, 2016.

While hiking to the summit I felt so weak. I felt so out of shape as I struggled up the mountain. "I'm not showing you a weakness" God said, "I'm showing you a strength. Your tenacity". I replayed the hike but with a different mind set. I had no misgivings about how hard that walk was going to be. I knew I was going to get dirty and was going to feel sick. I went anyway. I feel down and felt like I was going to throw up. But I never thought, "I'll stop and turn back". When it felt like my legs may not take another step I thought, "I'll crawl". I saw my Father's delight that I could be so determined; that I would persevere. That is what God wants from us; to not just run up the mountains we can easily skip and jump across, but to be so determined and steadfast, we follow Him up the hard ones. After all, he hiked the hardest hill for us. The journey up is worth it and the sunrise looks so much better from a summit.
