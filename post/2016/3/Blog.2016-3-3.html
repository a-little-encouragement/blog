The Stray

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 3rd, 2016.

When I was 17 I got a kitten. He had been a stray found under my porch and taken inside. I tried to pet him and he hissed. But I was determined. At one point he forgot I was there and wandered close to me. I reached out to grab him, expecting him to bite or scratch. Instead he melted. He started purring and snuggled up to me. After that he became my shadow. He never wanted to be separated from me. I recall someone trying to take him off my lap and he sank his claws in to my jeans. He refused to leave even to get a treat. In the same way Jesus Christ pursued me despite my protest. He snatched me up and in his hand I found everything I never realized I wanted and needed. I will not be removed.

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
