Selfless

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 7th, 2016.

I saw this photo on Facebook the other day.
I have other intentions for my year.

2016 will be a selfless year.
My time will be invested in others.
On improving the world.
I want to be broken bread and poured out wine.
I don't want to be a container.
I want to be a spring.
A bucket free to be sent to fetch the living water that flows from heaven.
A light reflecting Jesus Christ.
We (as a society) are always so focus on ourselves. When all we do is take in and become stagnant we become like the dead sea. Only bodies of water that flow contain life. Yes, there is a time to take care of ourselves and being selfless and helping others doesn't always look the way others think it might. But our individualistic society has long passed the extreme of self-focus. As a Christian, I'm called to a different path.

Verse:

For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand, that we should walk in them. - Ephesians 2:10

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
