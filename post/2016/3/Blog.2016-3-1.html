Tales From The Mountains Pt. 1: The Tree

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 1st, 2016.

I was at a women's retreat this weekend and ended up hiking Saturday to a mountain summit. While there, a friend of mine knew she needed to see the sunrise from that summit. So at six in the morning we walked back up there. At one steep point, towards the end, my friend knew that if I stopped then I wouldn't make it. She pointed out a tree just at the top of this treacherous part and said, "just get to that tree. Get to that tree then you can rest". I moved faster than I had the whole trip. I ran to that tree and wrapped my arms around it. I held on to that tree as I rested. Shortly after, while watching the sunrise from the summit, God brought that tree to mind and said, "Dallas, I am the tree." Just as that tree became a focal point that allowed me to moved faster so is Jesus to our lives. He is my focal point; my goal.
