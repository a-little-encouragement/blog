On The Throne

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 8th, 2016.

One day I had this vision. I was on the throne of Jesus in his lap. It was gold and high above everything. I was relaxing against his chest when beast, like wild dogs, starting barking (inadequate word to describe the noise they made). Jesus paid them no mind so I tugged his shirt. He looked down but he wasn't concerned. Then, very matter-of-factly he said, "Tell them to go. They'll go."

I'd like to point out some things. First Jesus didn't give me anything new. I already had the authority to tell them to go, I just needed to use it. Second, this authority did not come from me, it came from Him. It is for every believer. Not something given to me cause I'm a special. Third, I was perfectly safe where I was. Yes,the noise was annoying but the beast couldn't even get close to that throne.

Verse:

What, then, shall we say in response to these things? If God is for us, who can be against us? - Romans 8:31

About
