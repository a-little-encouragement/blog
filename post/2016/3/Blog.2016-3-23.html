Impatience & Fear Pt. 3: Free Will

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 23nd, 2016. Part 1 → Part 2 →

This is a three part series of reflections and summaries of this talk by Kim Walker-Smith.

Kim Walker Smith - Impatience & Fear

Have you ever thought about your free will as a weapon? The enemy really does not want us to realize is that it is. The enemy doesn't over ride our free will. He works by convincing us to make bad choices. Every time we choose to listen to God instead we walk under his protection. Hell trembles at a person determined to do the will of the Father.
