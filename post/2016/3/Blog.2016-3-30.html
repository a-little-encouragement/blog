Common Confusion:

Written by Dallas Pepper on March 30th, 2016.

There are some things that are commonly confused. In this series I will attempt to address some of them.

There is a big difference between love and affection. Love is not feeling it is an action. Affection is a feeling. Affection is dependent on a condition. Love is not. Emotions change. Affection comes and goes and comes back again. Love does not. God doesn't just have affection for us. He loves us. The action of the cross proves it.

Greater love has no one than this: to lay down one's life for one's friends. - John 15:13
