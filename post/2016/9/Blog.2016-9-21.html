The Right Side

Written by Dallas Pepper on September 21st, 2016.

Commander Harken: "Seems odd you'd name your ship after a battle you were on the wrong side of."
Captain Reynolds: "May have been the losing side. Still not convinced it was the wrong one."
- FireFly Ep. 2 Bushwhacked

There is going to be times that being on God's side feels like the losing one. Despite winning the war there are going to be battles we lose or at least feel that way. We have to know that as followers of Jesus. Satan is good at making us feel that every little scratch is some major setback. There are going to be loses that really are devastating and embarrassing. Which means there are going to be times that, like {Mal Reynolds}, you're going to need to stand firm in the face of scoffers knowing you are on the right side.

Remember the disciples didn't get what Jesus's mission was before the cross. They didn't know that chapter of the story was coming. Most Jews at that time expected that the Messiah was going to come riding in as a powerful warrior, free them from Roman rule and reestablish their kingdom. Calvary felt like such a huge defeat. It must have been very devastating and confusing.

We are on God's side and regardless of how things feel or appear at one moment He has all eternity in his hand. Spoiler alert, we win.

About
