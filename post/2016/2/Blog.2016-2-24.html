If We're Honest

Written by Dallas Pepper on February 24th, 2016.

'Your wrath lies heavily upon me
Your wrath has swept over me
Your terrors have destroyed me'
I mean seriously do you say that kind of stuff to God?...
Maybe you should.
- Steffany Gretzinger

To be truly intimate with anyone you have to be real. It is no different with God. He wants our real and raw emotion. He can take it. He already knows. We don't fool him for a minute so why bother pretending. It's okay to go to God angry and in pain. Holding on and trying to hide what he already sees just makes it harder for him to work with and bring us through. Give it to Him. This is also how we can be able to minister to others, with open hearts.

from The word In Life study Bible
