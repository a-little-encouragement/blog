Napkins And Water Bottles

Written by Dallas Pepper on February 5th, 2016.
"I know the state of your fickle flesh, better than you do. But I also know the state of your heart."

These were the words spoken to me after a night I messed up. I did something I knew I shouldn't have done and I was beating myself up. It still didn't really sink in though.

Then He spoke again: "I expect you to eat the napkins and chew up the water bottles" That sounds really strange but God knows me. You see, I have a friend who has a giant(95lbs) three year old golden retriever poodle mix named Silas. Two of Silas's favorite things are napkins and water bottles. Despite Silas's bad habits I love him. I'm happy to see his big puppy face when ever I go over. I do not expect him to be something other than what he is. I expect him to eat my napkin and run off with my water bottle. Just like how I know dogs, and that particular dog, God knows people and He knows me. He did make me after all. I never take him by surprise.
