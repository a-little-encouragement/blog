Being Gideon

Written by Dallas Pepper on February 25th, 2016.

I've spent several nights (and several days) begging God to give me more signs that I've heard something he said correctly. I wanted another dream or vision or something. I felt like Gideon with his fleece. God asked me if I had another dream would I be satisfied and stopped having doubts. I replied honestly with, "probably not". I turned to the book of judges and found this note by the chapters about Gideon. It is a common thing I think for us. We want to do God's will but we worry we don't know what it is. I think the most important thing is to not let the fear paralyze us. I've also come to believe that God can and will correct our course if we happen to start going the wrong way, if our eyes and hearts are on his. He is also sweet and gracious. I did have a dream. Do I still have my moments of doubt? Yep.

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
