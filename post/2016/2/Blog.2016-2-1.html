Lullabies

Written by Dallas Pepper on February 1st, 2016.

No one writes better love songs than our God. Sometimes it is a fast paced number I can dance to and often it is a lullaby. Whatever the melody over me, it is always of love.
One night I was enjoying His lullaby in a small cabin bunk. I could feel His presence hovering over my bed and even at one point by my hair. The song was to the beat of my heart. The next day I went for listening prayer. The first person to speak said that he just heard how much God delights to be with me. I don't have to speak. He got a picture of God sitting over my bed just enjoying the sound of my heart beat.
God loves each of us. Not "us" as in the human race but each of us individually. He loves you. You mean the world to Him. He thinks about you all the time and he knows you even better than you do. Every little detail. His love is wild and passionate and He never loves half-hearted.

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
