Gifts

Written by Dallas Pepper on February 19th, 2016.

I know Paul said there are benefits to being single but, as a single mom, I wasn't feeling it. I had the head knowledge that God's ways are best but not the heart knowledge. Deep down part of me was still thinking I can do so much more if I had a husband. I viewed this season as something to endure. Then someone challenged that view. Singleness is a gift. Every season is. How much better life is when we remember that. Now that I'm further along this season I can look back and see ways in which God really did know best. It has brought me closer to God and that is priceless.

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
