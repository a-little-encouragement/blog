The Tree

Written by Dallas Pepper on May 23rd, 2016.

A girl found a patch of desert land where a seed had been planted. Her Master instructed her to take care of it so she did. She watered the seed and took care of the ground. Every one around mocked her. They didn't believe anything could grow in that spot. Despite their taunts she stayed. Despite the harsh heat and storms she stayed. She had been promised something would grow. Year after year nothing grew. Then in the sixth year a small sprout broke through the earth. Very quickly this sprout became a mighty tree. The girl sat under its shade and enjoyed the wonderful fruit it produced. The people who had mocked her came and wanted some of the fruit too. They asked for some and the girl replied, "As it was done unto me, so I'll do to you." Then she gave them plenty of fruit and let them sit in the shade. They didn't understand these things for they didn't know she was talking about her Master. The tree continued to grow under her care. Other people joined in the work. The tree continued to thrive for a long time; long after the girl was gone.

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
