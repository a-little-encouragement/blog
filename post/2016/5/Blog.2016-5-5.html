Healing Testimony

Written by Dallas Pepper on May 26th, 2016.

We don't always see the fruit of our prayers right away. Here is one example. When I was a very young child I was diagnosed with a genetic defect that could cause me all sorts of problems. I was given a uncertain future; heart problems, kidney problems, learning problems. One thing they thought was certain was I would never have children. I didn't grow up in church, but I do remember someone taking me not long after my diagnosis. I don't remember anything about the service except the end. To my horror when they gave the call for prayer I was picked up and taken to the alter. Some strangers prayed for me and I don't think they ever saw me again. There was no big magically moment where God confirmed He answered their prayer. But, each year, I developed fine. I even had a son. Regardless of whether our prayers get answered in the way we would like, they are very important and never ignored by God.
