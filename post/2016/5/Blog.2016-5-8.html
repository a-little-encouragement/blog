Mothers' Day

Written by Dallas Pepper on May 8th, 2016.

Happy mothers' day! It is a hard job that, like many moms, I often don't feel very good at. Here is an encouraging video for all the others feeling the same way. I also wrote down my reflections on being a single mother.

A New Perspective for Moms

Being a single mom is a hard road to walk. It is made harder by the fact most people do not plan on walking it and usually find it through traumatic circumstances. However, like many dark circumstances, it can serve as a back drop to show off the brightest of God's love and depths of His grace. I've been a single mom for over five years now. Not once has he ever dropped me. The journey, though hard has brought me closer. Not having something else, or someone else to cling to has caused me to grip very tightly to Jesus 's hand. Through my journey I've learned He is a wonderful artist that waste nothing. Whether it be the offenses of others, seemingly random tragedy, or my own mistakes God has used it for good and His glory. That is the God I serve who calls me his own; the maker of beauty from ashes.
