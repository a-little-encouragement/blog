Able and Willing

Written by Dallas Pepper on December 23rd, 2016.

I've been convinced of God's ability to do anything for a long time. His willingness to is another story. It is still sometimes hard to believe that the God who create the whole universe cares about my personal happiness. Since we are broken people, living in a broken world, it is easy to forget that God is a loving father that wants to pour out extravagant gifts on us. Those gifts don't always look like what we think they should. Those gifts don't always look like bags of gold, a perfect house, and perfect relationships. Sometimes they look like a baby lying in a manger. Sometimes they look like a man on a cross. Sometimes they look like "no" because God knows things we don't. God isn't after His personal interested in spite of ours. If that was true He wouldn't have made us in the first place(He knew what we would do). Eve ate the fruit because she believed the lie that God didn't have her best interest in mind. The truth is so easy to know with our mind, and so hard to believe with our hearts. If we, being imperfect, care about our children's well-being and are pained when they experience pain, how much more does God care about our well-being and how much more does He hurt at our suffering? This is the truth, I do not serve an apathetic God.

About
