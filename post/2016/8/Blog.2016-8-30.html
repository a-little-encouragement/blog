As Yourself

Written by Dallas Pepper on August 30th, 2016.

Scripture says love your neighbor as yourself. I think for many people that is exactly the problem. If you are filled with self-loathing how does that equation work out? 1 John says God is love. Not "God does love". Love is not something God feels or does, it is His very substance. Father, Son, Holy Spirit all together as one through love. That's right, God loves himself. Jesus said He loves the Father and the Father loves and delights in Him. We are created in His image. We are created by love, through love, in love. You are free to love yourself. God didn't work hard on you for you to tell Him his work is crap. It is not conceited. That is a lie from Satan. Real humility is accepting truth, not self-hatred. I don't believe we are capable of truly loving others if we don't love ourselves. I don't believe we are capable of viewing others as Christ does if we can't do it for us. I don't believe we can love ourselves if we do not comprehend God's love for us. In the story of the prodigal son, the son came home asking to just be a slave in his father's house. His father however immediately restored full family status back. Some of us have come home and are acting like a servant rather than a son or daughter. To God you are a special treasure, a labor of love, a masterpiece of art, and He loves you dearly. Try doing the same.
