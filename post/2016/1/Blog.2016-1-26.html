Written by Dallas Pepper on January 26th, 2016.

Did you not...? Are you not...? Will you not..? Before David fought Goliath Saul gave him his armor. But David refused it because he had not tested it. David didn't have history with armor. He wasn't use to moving in it and he wasn't going go put his faith in it. But David did have a history with God. David knew he could count on God in the fight with Goliath because he had tested God and knew he was faithful. In the same way, we can look back at our history with God and know he can be trusted and worldly things cannot. We can be bold giant slayers too. Did God not save us in the past? Is he not the same God today? Will he not save us now?

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
