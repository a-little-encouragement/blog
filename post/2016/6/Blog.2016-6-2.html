Calling

Written by Dallas Pepper on June 2nd, 2016.

What is my calling?
Most people ask this question. Understandably they want to know. When you see other Christians that seem to have a very clear purpose it is easy to feel like we should know. Truth is, a lot of us only know in hindsight or at least when we get there. It often doesn't feel like some special act just life. An even more important truth is people and their lives are complicated, multifaceted things, and that is the way God uses them. I don't think anyone just has one purposes. Even one act can have many so a lifetime has billions. Seek first his kingdom, then everything else will be added, including purpose. God doesn't want to be lost to any work which is why things are revealed in pieces. He wants a relationship not just to give you marching orders. Also take comfort in the fact that you can not miss your calling. Just stay focused on Jesus and trust He is working through you even in the everyday task.

Verses:

And I am certain that God, who began the good work within you, will continue his work until it is finally finished on the day when Christ Jesus returns. - Philippians 1:6

But seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you. - Matthew 6:33

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
