Salt and Light

Written by Dallas Pepper on November 4th, 2016.

Things don't look very great in our country right now. We don't know what will happen but God does. He is still in charge and he doesn't fall off of his throne. He isn't shocked and freaking out. Remember when the Ark of the Covenant got captured. It was probably one of the scariest times for the nation especially that anyone alive at the time had been through. Unseen to the Israelites the Philistines were having to scrape their broken "god" of the floor. They didn't have to do anything. They woke up and the arc was back (with gifts).
Right now especially we need to be the salt and light of the world. It doesn't take a stadium flood light to illuminate a corner and it doesn't take a pound of salt to season a steak. It only takes a little. It doesn't take a sophisticated argument, sermon, or grand gesture. A simple smile and kind word can be just what the world needs.
