Our Testimony

Written by Dallas Pepper on November 18th, 2016.

One thing I strive for with this blog is to be authentic. Authenticity can be hard because it requires vulnerability. Truth is always what people need. Scripture says we over come by the blood of the lamb and the word if our testimony. Yet many Christians aren't telling their testimony. Or they are telling such a cleaned up version it has become powerless. It is very hard to exalt God and exalt ourselves at the same time. To tell of the great things he has done we have to admit that we were so weak and broken we needed Him. If I have to be humbled to show off God so be it. I'll happily move out of the way, and be brought low, to see Him lifted up.

About

This started as a series of emails to a small group of my sweet sisters and quickly grew. I heard a pastor once say that he was preaching to himself. We were just listening in to what God had been working personally in him. That is what this blog is. It is a window into what God has been working in me. I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
