Wedding Bells

Written by Dallas Pepper on February 10th, 2016.

Over the weekend I took a road trip and felt like I heard the following riding through the mountains:

I blow the horn
I proclaim the coming of my Master
Make yourself ready
All of you who call yourself his bride!
Prepare for the wedding feast
Turn from the world
It is fleeting and ending
Turn towards Him
He is coming for his own
Keep the lamps lit
Yes, keep the torches going
May the night guard stay awake
Yes keep a vigil in the night
He has prepared for you a room
Don't bother going back to pack your belongings
There is no time!
Hear Him call
"Come away with me my love"
Answer swiftly
Say yes!
The wedding bells are ringing!
Listen!
He is waiting in anticipation!
He is waiting for His bride
Looking down the aisle
Waiting to see her beauty
She is walking down the aisle
In anticipation
Longing to see His splendor
The glory of His face
The love in His eyes
The smile on His face
Oh what a wonderful day!

About

I pray this will help encourage other believers as they run their own race.
