Keep Your Guard Up

Written by Dallas Pepper on May 29th, 2017.

"You know you are being attacked in that area. Why are you leaving it unguarded?"

We are told to put on the full armor of God. But armor is only preventative and it isn't permanent. Armor prevents or lessens injury, it doesn't heal injuries once they are inflicted. Jesus is the Great Physician that can heal all wounds. But isn't it so much better to not have those injuries in the first place? Putting on armor once doesn't protect you for all time. It has to be put on everyday, before every battle. After being reminded of these things God asked me, "Why are you unguarded?"

Not only was I not always putting on the general protections, I was leaving areas I knew would probably be attacked unprotected. If you know you are going to interact with someone who has a habit of offending you and throwing you off spiritually, guard yourself. If you know the enemy has been moving through your work place or family or church attempting to create division, guard yourself. If you know a certain temptation might lie ahead this day, guard yourself. Seems obvious yet I wasn't always doing this. Instead I was limping to God with injuries from attacks I could've seen coming miles away with a few seconds of thought. I do not believe I am the only person that has done this.
What does it look like to guard yourself? What does it look like to put on the armor of God? Prayer.
Discuss with God spiritual strategy to get you through the day. Pray asking for protection, reminding yourself of the past, and pre-deciding the future.
Pre-decide anything that person says doesn't matter. Pre-decide you won't respond with reactive rage rather than rationality. Pre-decide you are going to meet your family or coworker or church member with patience and kindness. Pre-decide you are not going to do that thing today.

Prayer/Declaration

Lord, I put on your belt of truth. You are the all-powerful creator and I am Your beloved. You have ransomed my life from sin. I wrap the truth of who You are, who I am, and what You have done for me as the substance that holds everything together.
I put on Your righteousness, underserved, and obtained by grace through faith. I am covered and cleansed by the blood of Jesus. My heart is free of guilt and shame. I will not accept accusation to the contrary by the enemy.
My feet are ready to carry the Gospel of peace. What ever rough terrain may come my way my feet are secure on the foundation of the Gospel. I will go where ever you call me to deliver that Good News to others.
I pick up the shield of faith. You are who You say you are and will do what You say You will do. Your ways are good and wise even when I do not understand them.
I guard my mind with the your salvation. I take every thought captive. Your salvation and eternal life through Jesus Christ is truth and I reject any doctrine, emotion, or deception that says otherwise.
I pick up the word of God, the sword of the spirit, with which I am able to deflect and counter the attacks of the enemy. I will study Your Word and hold it as the ultimate authority of truth. I cut down all strong holds and demolish all arguments that sets itself up against the knowledge of God.
